🎢Park Strategies

🏰 Magic Kingdom — Wed, Oct 14

  • Early Entry 8:30 AM — head to TRON Lightcycle Run or Seven Dwarfs first
  • Set 7:00 AM alarm — book LL Multi Pass the moment it opens
  • First pick: Peter Pan's Flight (sells out fast). LLSP for TRON if Mario hits 40"
  • Mario's rides: Dumbo, Barnstormer, Under the Sea, Buzz, Small World, Haunted Mansion
  • Midday break 1–4 PM at resort, return for parade and fireworks
  • CHECK: If MNSSHP falls Oct 12, regular guests cleared by 6–7 PM

🦁 Animal Kingdom — Mon, Oct 12

  • Rope drop is critical — Kilimanjaro Safari animals most active in morning
  • Book LLMP at 7:00 AM for Avatar Flight of Passage (adults only, 44" min)
  • Mario's picks: TriceraTop Spin, The Boneyard, Festival of the Lion King
  • AK closes earlier (~7–8 PM) — full morning, easy afternoon

🚀 Hollywood Studios — Tue, Oct 13

  • Easiest travel day — Skyliner gondola from Art of Animation right to entrance
  • Early Entry 8:30 AM — Slinky Dog Dash in Toy Story Land (38" min)
  • Book LLMP at 7:00 AM for Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run
  • Buy LLSP for Rise of the Resistance — best ride in WDW
  • Mario's Toy Story Land: Slinky Dog, Alien Swirling Saucers, Toy Story Mania

🍄 Epic Universe — Fri, Oct 16

  • Uber from Art of Animation — ~15–20 min, ~$20 each way
  • Night before: Universal app, secure Super Nintendo World virtual queue
  • Buy Express Pass Unlimited (~$130–200/person) — no early entry off-site
  • First ride: Mario Kart: Bowser's Challenge — longest queue in the park
  • Mario's day: Yoshi's Adventure (no height req) + Power-Up Band Block interactions
  • CRITICAL: Mario Kart requires 40" — measure your child before the trip
⏰7 AM rule: Set an alarm every Disney park morning. Lightning Lane Multi Pass drops at exactly 7:00 AM and the best slots disappear within minutes.

⚡Skip-the-Line Guide

MagicBand+ ~$34.99 each

Park entry, room key, charges, PhotoPass. Buy online before arrival — link to My Disney Experience for all 3 family members.

LL Multi Pass ~$15–30/day

Book 1 ride at a time, rebook after tap-in or 2 hrs. Set 7 AM alarm every Disney park morning.

LL Single Pass ~$10–25/ride

Skip the line on TRON (MK) and Rise of the Resistance (HS). Worth it — saves 90–120 min per ride.

Epic Express Pass Unlimited ~$130–200/person

Unlimited skip-the-line at Epic Universe. Primary tool — there's no early entry at Epic when staying off-site.
